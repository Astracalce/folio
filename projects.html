" Scene is a gripping documentary that takes viewers on a journey through the harsh realities of life in the savannah jungle. The film follows the story of a man who is one of the few remaining members of his tribe and must fight for survival against other tribes and dangerous wild creatures. Through stunning visuals and interviews with experts, Scene exposes the challenges faced by indigenous communities in the face of modernization and environmental degradation. This powerful film will leave viewers with a deeper understanding of the importance of preserving traditional ways of life and protecting our planet's precious ecosystems. "

Poster breakdown : The blueish toned poster designed for Netflix captures the intensity and rawness of this unforgettable story. As blue and green are the closest to nature i had used the blue for this one. The main title scene, which is almost visible gives some dark tone to the poster and the blood dripping from the eye of the protagnist also gives the vibe of some thrill.
